Welly Mage

The game created was a Pacman like game which took inspiration from core concepts of Pacman. The game is not a copycat of Pacman as it has had its core mechanics changed. The game is from the top-down perspective of a skeleton who is searching for his lost bones and orbs, however, there are ghosts which move across the screen whilst the player tries collecting their bones and if the player gets hit, then they will lose the game.

This was my first unity project in which I learned alot about the different functions in Unity and large range of programming techniques. A some of the sprites that were used were found online, however the player sprite sheet was made first hand using the free online pixel art website called Piskel

The bones and orbs that the player must collect in order to increase their score spawn randomly around the level.
